Transcriptomics

Transcriptomics

  • Reference Expression: Collection of gene expression in different tissues of 8 genomes

  • Population Expression: Collection of population-level gene expression in maize association mapping panel:

  • Kernel: expression of 42,116 genes in 340 germplasms

  • Leaf: expression of 43,804 genes under 3 conditions in 225 germplasms

  • Transcript factor binding sites: Collection of Chip-Seq data of 104 transcript factor in maize based on B73v4
